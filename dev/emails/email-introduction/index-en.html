Introducing the Lucozade Sport Euro Sweepstake

Sign Up & Win!

We're inviting you to take part in our Lucozade Sport Women's Euro Sweepstake. We're bringing a new, fresh, and exciting experience into the palm of your hands during all 31 games.

You can see:

  • how you rank amongst other colleagues
  • your team selected and their game stats
  • upcoming team games
  • start-to-end match score history
  • and, real-time results for every game

 

How to Take Part

 

Step 1

Complete your Team Selection: Complete your team selection form, choosing one team from each group. You will select 3 teams from any group, + 2 random teams. Once submitted, your selected teams cannot be changed.

 

 
 

Step 2

Get your Dashboard: After completing your team selection form, you'll be able to access your dashboard. Visit euros.sbfe.digital or Scan the QR Code and get ready to launch your Sweepstake Dashboard.

Pssst! We've designed the dashboard for mobile, meaning it'll be best used in the palm of your hand!

 

Step 3

Add your Email: To setup the dashboard and make personal to you, add your Suntory email address as the 'Viewer'. This will complete the setup, personalising the dashboard for you and your Euros team selection.
