Wood Street Capital

Wood Street Capital are an independent UK based turnaround investor and private equity specialist focusing on financial investment and the provision of hands-on operational expertise to businesses facing stress, distress or other special situations.

We received an email from a marketing manager in the middle of March 2014, asking for complete branding and website design for a new up and coming business investor. We loved the sound of that.

After hours of designing and collaboration with their partners and marketing manager, we united Wood Street Capital with their solid and meaningful brand. Delivering their message of love for capitalising turnaround and growth.

Agreeing their logo design and guidelines, a large array of brand materials were designed. Such as business cards, CD covers and sleeves, letterhead, compliment slips, envelops, folders, invoice and quotations, seasonal greeting card, HTML marketing templates, iOS home screen icon, stickers and much more.

To tell the truth, we really enjoy working with startups just as much as an already up and running organisation. We love to see and hear their passion and excitement, and really get a kick out of seeing them succeed.
